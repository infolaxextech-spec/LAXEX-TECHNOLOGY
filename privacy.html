Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 2, 2026  ·  laxextech.com

We believe in full transparency. Here is exactly how we handle your information.

01

Introduction

Laxex Technology ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information you share with us when you visit laxextech.com or contact us for our services.

We are a digital agency based in Gujarat, India. We take your privacy seriously and have built our website in a way that collects as little data as possible.

✅ Short version — we collect zero data automatically. The only info we receive is what you voluntarily send us via WhatsApp.
02

Information We Collect

We do not run any backend server or database on this website. The only way we receive your information is when you voluntarily fill out our contact or enquiry form. That form does not store your data anywhere — it simply opens a pre-filled WhatsApp message that you send directly to us.

The information you may choose to share includes:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number or email (if you mention it in your message)
  • Your project enquiry or message content
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you send us only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry promptly
  • Understand and discuss your project requirements
  • Send you a quotation or proposal
  • Follow up on an ongoing project or conversation

We do not use your information for marketing, remarketing, or advertising purposes without your explicit consent.

04

How We Do NOT Use Your Information

We will never:

  • Sell your personal information to anyone, ever
  • Share your data with third-party companies or advertisers
  • Store your information in any database or CRM without your knowledge
  • Send you unsolicited promotional messages or spam
  • Use your project details or business information for our own benefit
✅ Your information stays between you and us — period.
05

WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via our website form, your message is sent through WhatsApp directly to our business number. This means:

  • Your message is governed by WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy — whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • We receive and read your message only on our WhatsApp account
  • WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption, so your message is secure in transit
  • We do not screenshot, forward, or share your conversation without your permission
ℹ️ By using our contact form, you are choosing to initiate a WhatsApp conversation with us. We only respond — we never initiate unsolicited contact.
06

Cookies & Tracking

Our website currently does not use:

  • Cookies of any kind (session, persistent, or third-party)
  • Analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Hotjar
  • Meta Pixel, Google Ads tags, or any retargeting scripts
  • Tracking pixels or fingerprinting techniques
  • Any third-party advertising or data collection scripts
✅ Your browsing on laxextech.com is completely untracked and anonymous.
07

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as Instagram, WhatsApp, or client project previews. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

  • Instagram — Instagram Privacy Policy
  • WhatsApp — WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — used for typography only, no tracking

We recommend reading the privacy policies of any external sites you visit from our website.

08

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors under the age of 18.

If you believe a minor has contacted us through our website, please notify us immediately at info.laxextech@gmail.com and we will delete that information right away.

09

Data Security

Since we do not store any user data on servers or databases, the risk of a data breach on our end is virtually zero. Here is how your data stays safe:

  • Our website has no backend — no server processes or stores your form data
  • WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption for all messages
  • All client project files shared with us are handled confidentially
  • We do not store client credentials, passwords, or payment card details
✅ No database = no data breach risk from our side.
10

Your Rights

Even though we collect minimal data, you have the following rights at all times:

  • Right to know — ask us what information we have received from you
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of any conversation or data related to you
  • Right to opt out — stop follow-up communication from us at any time
  • Right to correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate information

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us via WhatsApp or email and we will respond within 48 hours.

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change

  • Continued use of our website after changes means you accept the updated policy
